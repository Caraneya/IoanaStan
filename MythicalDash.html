Mythical Dash

MYTHICAL DASH

SIDE-SCROLLING MULTIPLAYER ARCADE - DESIGNER

OVERALL

This colorful game brings together elements from various mythologies where only the fastest can survive.

By introducing the idea of making a game both physically and digitally, the creation process required a new, more technical approach.

While it can be played even on a computer, it is highly recommended to use the custom input device for a complete fun experience.

STORY

Explore various mythologies and run from Mount Olympus to the Pyramids, while avoiding obstacles and angry gods.

Play as Ajax, a Greek who loved Prometheus and stole the Lyra from Apollo, so he can share its music with everyone. Or play as Masika, an Egyptian priestess who stole a sickle from Osiris to help her people.

But now, both characters are running for their lives as the Gods are trying to punish them!

INPUT DEVICE

Designing the input device presented new challenges, including learning how to laser cut and creating an easy-to-assemble design.

I had to take into account very strict measurements and the result reached the expectations.

It was a unique experience to make something that could control the game and it was a welcome change from constantly looking at a screen.
