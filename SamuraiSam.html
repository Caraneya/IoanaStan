Samurai Sam

SAMURAI SAM

2D POINT AND CLICK GAME - DESIGNER

OVERALL

Samurai Sam was an interesting and challenging initiation in game design. Applying principles of design thinking and project management techniques, our team developed this pixelated point and click game.

As a designer, I was in charge of the game's concept, storyline, and mechanics. In terms of visual elements, I was involved in the creation of logos, environmental designs, and promotional materials, including its poster and trailer.

STORY

This game presents a unique experience where you play with Sam, a samurai that left his old days of glory and started working for his father's business.

While delivering and walking around, Sam uncovers intriguing personal and familial secrets.

Players should keep in mind that their decisions have consequences, and they should never overlook the importance of petting dogs.

PRODUCT DESIGN

In designing the poster, my intention was to highlight the characters, with Samurai Sam taking center stage.

The purpose of the bottom design with the restaurant was to incorporate the game setting and how it actually looks like while playing it.

To distinguish between these two elements, I added mountains which not only provided a visually appealing solution, but also created a V-shaped layout for the top portion.

TRAILER

When creating the game trailer, I first drafted a storyboard that included all necessary sounds and specific details for each scene.

I followed the storyboard closely and added comic-style sounds to align with the game's aesthetic and make the trailer relatable to the target audience.

The video followed a classic structure, introducing the background story of the main character, showcasing his in-game objectives, and concluding with a call to action to promote it.
