Slanster Cats

SLANGSTER CATS

2D TYPING GAME - LEAD DESIGNER

OVERALL

In this comic game, you play as Millie, a cat who's been picked on too many times for not knowing enough slang.

You fight your way through rough neighborhoods by rapping, aiming to expand your vocabulary and master new slang.

But rapping is not easy, success in these battles requires quick typing skills, precise word choice, and a solid grasp of the latest lingo.

CONCEPT

You play as Millie, who must prove her worth by rap battling other cats. She has to show her mastery of slang, and that she belongs there.

The gameplay involves typing the correct slang to deal damage to opponents and checking the dictionary where new words and definitions are added. Winning earns Reputation Points that unlock new levels.

Currently, the game takes place on the dangerous London streets, but team BLEEEH hopes to add to it more zones and slang varieties.

MENU INTERFACE

The UI design is consistent with the comical and childish style of the game, with menus shaped like a cat paw and buttons placed playfully on their pink pads, or on the fish-shaped cracker.

For the typography, Modak and Bubblegum Sans were chosen for their resemblance to graffiti tags.

The background was darkened to create a sense of depth and features the team members' tags, as well as a popular character: Aeul, made by a Romanian graffiti artist.

HUD DESIGN

The in-game interface needed to blend with its surroundings, so I used a dog tag as the Reputation Points(RP) container, and a dictionary icon I designed specifically to fit with the overall style.

Like in all the animations before, I used Unity Timeline for the VS screen, and I created a a bouncy, rubber-like effect to resemble a classic cartoon moment.

CUTSCENE EDIT

By using DaVinci Resolve I edited this amusing cutscene, taking some time just to listen to cats meowing sounds, and choose the best ones.
