Galactic Express

GALACTIC EXPRESS

2D PHYSICS-BASED PUZZLE GAME - LEAD DESIGNER

OVERALL

This game challenges its players to solve twisted spacey puzzles to ensure successful deliveries throughout the whole galaxy!

With a focus on its extraterrestrial physics, players control Cillus, a small alien, as he shoots his packages directly to its fellow humorous aliens.

CONCEPT

Our team assigned Cillus with a special mission: it must find a way to deliver the orders safely and efficiently.

The alien species developed a very high-tech truck that can effectively throw any package anywhere, but it has one tiny flaw: the items are poorly packed. The cosmic spheres are prone to breaking!

Cillus needs to succeed, the whole galaxy depends on him!

USER INTERFACE

The desired atmosphere for the game was friendly and simple, so I created a collage with existing visuals.

I efficiently used already-made graphics to create different looks for the level selection and starting page.

The in-game interface was given a rounded design, with boxes featuring rounded corners and gradient effects based on the game's color palette, resulting in a calming yet interesting appearance.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Firstly prototyping it in Figma, then building it from the ground up in VS Code, the Galactic Express website showcases the game and its merch.

I aimed for a sleek appearance, with sections to showcase the game and its features. Implementing the various layouts required coding challenges, but I thoroughly enjoyed the process of building the website.
