HIGH INFINITY GUARD

BUSINESS WEBSITE - UI/UX DESIGNER

OVERALL

This design was made for a security company and was intended to reflect their focus on safety and modern equipment. The client desired a professional, cutting-edge website that would showcase their business values.

I prioritized both the aesthetic and practicality of this design, ensuring it met the client's request to be easily integrated into a website builder.

PROJECT

The client had a logo and content for a presentation, but no specific design in mind for their website. They asked for standard pages such as Careers, Services, and Contact.

I chose a modern look with sharp square corners and used a gray-blue color palette to reflect the high-end technology they offer.

USER INTERFACE & EXPERIENCE

I designed the homepage to include both the About Us and Services sections in a user-friendly, single-scroll layout.

I selected eye-catching images that complement the blue-tinted color scheme and used a consistent palette of varying shades of grey for the containers while adding a splash of blue to keep the user engaged.

On hover, the background color of the interactive elements changes to provide visual feedback to the user.

At the end of each section, a prominent call-to-action button leads the user to the website's most popular areas.

The contact form followed a classic structure, with clear input fields and a send button. When the button is pressed, an icon and a message indicate that the submission was successful, promoting a sense of security.
