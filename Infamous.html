OVERALL

Infamous is a clothing brand defined by interesting and controversial stories about its merch and its origins.

They asked for a variety of designs to embody their concepts, ranging from a logo to T-shirts and blouses. I created multiple designs and variations that followed their guidelines, investing significant attention and detail into them.

PROJECT

This project, carried out by commission, ranks among my most successful collaborations.

Even though it was challenging, as it was my first time working with a professional client, I managed to balance my time effectively and meet all the deadlines.

I learned the significance of clear and productive communication, as well as the importance of being receptive to feedback and iterating on the product continuously to meet the client's standards.

WORDMARK

I began my design process by carefully considering the brief provided by the client, including their specified monochromatic color palette.

I conducted thorough research into typefaces, presenting the client with three variations for consideration.

The first option was an italic font with a curved disorganized appearance (Merienda One), the second had a more elegant look (Amita), and the third was more rigid, reminiscent of a classic typewriter font (Philomene One).

After careful consideration, the client selected the first option, due to its compatibility with the curved design of the snake.

Then, I designed the snake and and experimented with various shadow and texture effects. I also utilized my own custom brush to achieve the desired grayscale gradient in the tree leaves.
