What services do you offer?

I offer a wide range of design services, including website design & development, UI/UX, product design, graphic design, game design and brand identity.

How much?

Prices vary based on effort, type of content, and number of variations desired. Contact me for more details and don't forget to include your brief.

What is your design process like?

1. Determine client goals & expectations
2. Create low-fi prototype or sketch the concept
3. Get feedback & choose approach
4. Add talent & some magic
5. Enjoy the freshly made design!

Can you work in a team setting?

Definitely! I love being part of a team and always come with a positive and creative vibe. My adaptability to different people makes me a great team player, and I am a human afterall, we are made to thrive and progress when we socialise.

Can you work with existing guidelines?

Yes, the clearer the brief is, the better for me as a designer. It gives me a great understanding of the client's vision and needs, and allows me to create the perfect design that fits them.

How do you prefer to communicate?

For most collaborations, I primarily use email as it's convenient for attaching large documents and I frequently check it.
