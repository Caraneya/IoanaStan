Impaired

IMPAIRED

3D WALKING SIMULATOR GAME - DESIGNER

OVERALL

This game was created by team Roadkill to raise awareness and support a campaign against impaired driving.

It uses psychological impact and dramatic effects to guilt-trip the player through their whole experience.

With the help of story-telling elemetns and an investigative approach the player finds clues that reveal the devastating consequences of driving under the influence.

STORY

The events begin with the player facing the difficult choice of whether to make a beer run for the party despite being impaired.

The whole experience is built on the principle of choice, if the player chooses not to drive twice, they receive a happy ending.

But if they get behind the wheel, they are shown the aftermath of a car crash and the only way to find help is to face their consequences.

USER INTERFACE

For this serious game, a suitable interface was required, so I went for a monochromatic palette with sharp square buttons and subtle white outlines.

The start page was inspired from existing racing games, tweaking the text boxes to look more dynamic.

My familiarity with Unity was very useful in this project. After prototyping the UI designs in Figma, I implemented them successfully in the Unity environment, which minimised the work load for the engineers and led to a smoother game development process.
